# Visual Text Chatbot 

**Visual Text Chatbot** connects ChatGPT and a series of Visual Foundation Models to enable **sending** and **receiving** images during chatting.

## Insight & Goal:
One the one hand, **Large Language Models** serves as a **general interface** that provides a broad and diverse understanding of a
wide range of topics. On the other hand, **Foundation Models** serve as **domain experts** by providing deep knowledge in specific domains.
By leveraging **both general and deep knowledge**, we aim at building an AI that is capable of handling a various of tasks.
